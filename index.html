  • Javascript helps you create really beautiful and crazy fast websites.You can develop your website with a console like look and feel and give your users the best graphical user experience.

  • JavaScript usage is now extented to mobile app Development,desktop app Development and game development.This opens many oppurtunities for you as Javascript Programmer

  • Due to high demand ,there is tons of job growth and high pay for those who know Javascript.you can navigate over to different Job sites to see what having Javascript skills look like in the Job market.

  • Great thing about Javascript is that you find tons of frameworks and libraries already developed which can be used directly in the software development to reduce you time consumed to complete the given task
